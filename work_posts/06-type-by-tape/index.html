Constantine Pitsilos

Type by Tape (Through Time)


Typeforce is an annual showcase of emerging typographic artists in Chicago. Nearly 1,000 people poured through the doors of Co-Prosperity Sphere, Chicagoâ€™s experimental cultural centre, for the opening night of Typeforce 4.

Our design collective wanted to explore interactivity and curiosity by creating an installation using analogue materials. At the start of the evening, our piece began as a large, empty wall with solid and dashed lines. Attendees were given rolls of tape and invited to cover the dashed lines.

As the night progressed the party worked together to solve the puzzle. For hours, it inspired conversation and encouraged thoughtful examination.
