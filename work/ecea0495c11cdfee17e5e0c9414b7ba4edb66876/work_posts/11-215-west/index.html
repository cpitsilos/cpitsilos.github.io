215 West

215 West is a luxury apartment complex located in Chicago’s Loop neighborhood.

Working with Valerio Dewalt Train Associates architecture firm, I created a visual identity, promotional materials, and website for 215 West. Depending on its location, the word mark can be simplified into the abbreviated 215 W. A contemporary serif typeface was chosen for headlines, referencing the new building in a historic neighborhood. Included with the minimal identity is photography of the neighborhood and amazing views from the building’s units.
