iMac Pro Films Leaders Week

To demonstrate the power of iMac Pro, Apple invited a group of renowned filmmakers, CG artists, and motion graphics designers to create a personal piece of work using its advanced technologies.

Our strategy was to create a portfolio website sharing the films. We kept the design simple and dark, allowing the work to be the focus. The page loads with a short compilation of the films playing on the massive iMac Pro display. The user gets a glimpse of each film on hover, enticing them to click into the project. A simple interaction opens a project page, sharing the final film and a behind-the-scenes video with additional information on the creation process.

For the campaign, we wanted to display moments of each film on the tool with which they were created. The imagery not only showcases the visionary work. It expresses the power of the iMac Pro as the ultimate creative partner.
