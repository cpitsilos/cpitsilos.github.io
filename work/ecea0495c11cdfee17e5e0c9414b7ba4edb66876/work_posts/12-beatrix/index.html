Beatrix

Beatrix is a neighborhood coffeehouse and restaurant with 4 locations in Chicago and its surrounding areas. The menu features healthy meets delicious options, and also includes an iconic coffee and pastry counter.

The Beatrix brand was build to sit alongside a warm, inviting space inspired by the owners’ favorite cafés. Fresh bakery items are labeled daily with signs handwritten by the barista’s. The design team collaborated with a veteran sign painter to create menus and visuals with their own personality.

Beatrix has grown to multiple locations with more on the way. The approachable brand fits comfortably and becomes a feature in every neighborhood it reaches.
