Curry Ops

In a single season, Steph Curry made 402 three pointers, set a historic league-record of 73 wins, and was given a unanimous MVP award.

This shoe was built for what comes next.

In partnership with the Under Armour design team, we sought to capture the essence of Steph Curry, and the performance of the Curry 3 shoe, through a branded experience called Curry Ops. We developed a design system using the camouflage motif on the shoe with an art direction that is unexpectedly bold and unpredictable like the MVP. We chose a dynamic color scheme referencing his speed and plan of attack on the court.

Curry Ops hosted two regulation sized basketball courts featuring 3-on-3 games with high ranking players from local Oakland High Schools as well as various engagements for fans including food trucks, a pop up barber shop, a mural wall by Bay Area artists, a 360Â° photo booth, and a mobile store with exclusive merchandise.
