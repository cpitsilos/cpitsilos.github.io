Nightingale

Nightingale is a nonprofit popup theater and meeting place. It shows experimental films and holds talks from filmmakers. As it does not have a permanent location, every month it adopts a new space for a five day exhibition.

For the visual identity, I adopted a restricted color palette of black and white, allowing scenes from the films to be the main feature on posters and advertisements for the exhibitions. The logo includes a simple type treatment and a symbol of an origami nightingale, representing the DIY origin of the organization.
