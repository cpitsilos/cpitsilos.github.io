3D Objekte Drucken

While attending Schule für Gestaltung Basel, I created a poster for an exhibition held at Museum für Gestaltung Zürich.

The exhibition featured works that were created using 3D printing methods. I wanted the poster to represent the individual pieces and the collection as a whole. At a distance the poster has an illusion of dimension, inviting passersby to come closer and see the details of the exhibition.

The poster was selected as an addition to the Chicago Design Archive in 2013 by AIGA Chicago and the Society of Typographic Arts.
