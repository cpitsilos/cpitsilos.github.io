iPhone Xs & Xr

The Interactive team at Apple was tasked with creating a new branded web experience for iPhone. This included overview pages to tell the Xs and Xr stories at a glance, pages for key features of iPhone, tech specs, and supplemental campaign assets.

The iPhone Xs and Xr pages share the same structure, but they are each totally different experiences.

At every instance, the iPhone Xs page alludes to the relationship between beautiful design and amazing innovation. Huge typography references the Super Retina OLED display, the largest ever on an iPhone. Subtle animations mimic interactivity on iOS. Large scale photography reminds us why iPhone is the most popular camera in the world.

The design of the iPhone Xr page reflects how brilliant the device is. Large scale typography is colorful like the aluminum and glass design of iPhone Xr and its Liquid Retina display. Subtle color changes hints at the six stunning new finishes. Vibrant and dynamic photography references the iPhone Xr bringing iPhone to more people.
