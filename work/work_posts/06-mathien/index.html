Constantine Pitsilos Mathien: UniqueMan

Mathien: UniqueMan

Indie artist and producer Chris Mathien exists in a diverse world of intersecting styles and unique musical landscapes, which serve as a platform for his songwriting.

As part of his reinvention, I created a new visual direction beginning with a debut solo record titled UniqueMan.

Mathien’s eclectic musical background results in the merging of genres in every song. I looked to his influences in soul, R&B, psychedelic, and pop music, and their album’s artwork, as inspiration to create a piece that represents Mathien’s sound.
