Curry Ops

#CurryOps was a branded experience to announce the launch of Stephen Curry’s third signature shoe with Under Armour, the Curry 3.

It hosted two regulation sized basketball courts featuring 3-on-3 games with high ranking players from local Oakland High Schools as well as various engagements for fans including food trucks, a pop up barber shop, a mural wall by Bay Area artists, a 360° photo booth, and a mobile store with exclusive merchandise.
